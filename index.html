The COMPOST HERO™ is easy to fill and turn.
Just follow these simple instructions.

Step One: Prepare your organics.

  • Spread your COMPOST HERO™ out flat on the ground.
  • Pile your leaves on the Compost Hero™.
  • Lightly water and mix your organics, the pile should be slightly moist like a wrung out sponge.

Step Two: Wrap up your organics.

  • Wrap: Pull the red handle up and over the top of your organics pile and anchor under foot.
  • Pull left and right sides together, pull tight and secure with the first straps.
  • Wrap or flip your COMPOST HERO™ with the wrap straps.
  • Buckle the COMPOST HERO™ and tighten.

Step Three: Easily turn your COMPOST HERO™

  • Use the straps to flip or roll your COMPOST HERO™.
  • Flip your COMPOST HERO™ weekly to aerate.
